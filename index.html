How It Works

Wrapping/Unwrapping: Your NFT is transferred into the contract where it is locked, and a fungible token is minted for you. Unwrapping reverses this process.

Approvals: Use the Approve function to authorize a specific address to manage a single BAYC NFT, or Set Approval For All to authorize an operator to manage all your BAYC NFTs.
